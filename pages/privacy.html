Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data BizLink collects, why it is collected, and how it is protected.

On This Page

  • 2. How We Use Data
  • 3. Data Sharing

1. Data We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, phone, and role.
  • Transaction and order information for marketplace operations.
  • Usage logs, device information, and security-related events.

2. How We Use Data

  • To operate account access, order processing, and vendor workflows.
  • To detect fraud, protect users, and maintain platform security.
  • To provide support, notifications, and service updates.

3. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with payment processors, delivery partners, and service providers only as required to operate the platform. BizLink does not sell personal data.

4. Data Security

BizLink uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards including access controls, encryption in transit, and activity monitoring.

No method of transmission is fully secure. Users should also protect their passwords and account devices.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for platform operations, legal obligations, and dispute resolution.

6. Your Rights

  • Request account data access or correction.
  • Request deletion where legally permissible.
  • Manage communication preferences in account settings.
