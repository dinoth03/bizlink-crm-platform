Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll adjust your billing accordingly.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment in full.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), debit cards, and local Sri Lankan payment methods including bank transfers and eZ Cash.
Is there a setup fee? +
No setup fees! You start with a free 14-day trial, then upgrade to your chosen plan when you're ready.
Can I import my existing data? +
Yes! The Professional and Enterprise plans include unlimited data import. Starter plan includes limited import. Our support team can help.
What about data security? +
We use industry-leading encryption, regular security audits, and comply with international data protection standards. Your data is safe with us.
