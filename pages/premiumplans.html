Frequently Asked Questions

Vendors: Your Questions Answered

What are featured listings and how do they help? +
Featured listings appear prominently on the marketplace homepage and category pages, giving your products maximum visibility. Growth Plan vendors get 2 featured listings per month; Enterprise sellers get unlimited.
How many products can I list as a Starter vendor? +
Starter vendors can list up to 50 products. Upgrade to Growth Plan or Enterprise Seller for unlimited product listings.
What kind of analytics will I get? +
Starter vendors get basic sales data. Growth Plan includes detailed analytics on views, clicks, conversions, and customer behavior. Enterprise sellers get custom analytics reports tailored to their business.
Can I manage customer relationships on BizLink? +
Growth Plan and Enterprise sellers get full CRM tools to track customer interactions, manage bulk orders, and build loyalty. Starter vendors can message customers directly through the platform.
What's included in the dedicated seller manager for Enterprise? +
Your dedicated manager helps optimize your shop setup, suggests growth strategies, troubleshoots issues, and ensures you get maximum value from BizLink. They're available for priority support calls.
Can I integrate my own systems with BizLink? +
API access is available only for Enterprise sellers, allowing you to integrate inventory, accounting, and fulfillment systems directly with BizLink.
Is there a setup fee? +
No setup fees! You start with a free 14-day trial, then upgrade to your chosen plan when you're ready.
Can I import my existing data? +
Yes! The Professional and Enterprise plans include unlimited data import. Starter plan includes limited import. Our support team can help.
What about data security? +
We use industry-leading encryption, regular security audits, and comply with international data protection standards. Your data is safe with us.
