About Us

BizLink is a modern CRM and business connection platform that helps local vendors, customers, and admins work from one trusted digital space. We simplify operations, improve communication, and help businesses scale with confidence.

Our Mission

To make digital business tools practical, affordable, and powerful for every business in Sri Lanka. We focus on real workflows: customer management, sales visibility, marketplace growth, and trusted communication.

Our Story

BizLink started as a student-led vision to solve day-to-day business pain points. Today, it is growing into a full business ecosystem where vendors can sell smarter, customers can discover better options, and admins can manage everything from one dashboard.

Trust First
